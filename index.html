Recent Projects

SoundCloud 2

A fullstack clone of SoundCloud, and my most comprehensive individual project to date. Users can sign in, upload, edit, and delete songs, and can browse and comment on songs from other users. Fully functional and personally-engineered audio player included, with a clickable progress bar and waveform visualization to choose what part of the song you want to listen to.


Technologies: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, React, Redux, CSS, Webpack, AWS, BCrypt, and Wavesurfer.js.

Social Q's

A team-based MERN stack social-polling application that aggregegates user-response data and allows for comparison with the rest of user-population. Users can sign in and create and answer 'Would You Rather' or 'Red-Flag or Dealbreaker' type questions, and can then analyze interactive data visualizations of how everyone responded broken down by gender and age.


Technologies: MongoDB, Express, React, Redux, Git Branching, Node.js, Chart.js, and CSS.

JS-1000

A personalized polyphonic synthesizer fully constructed with Vanilla Javascript and the Web Audio API; no other libraries used. Generate a base audio signal with your specified waveform, and modify that signal to create inspiring sounds using filters, effects, and a volume envelope. You can play the synth with your computer keyboard, or with your mouse. Corresponding key-map and run-down of the different components coming soon.


Technologies: JavaScript, the MDN Web Audio API, HTML, and CSS.

This app makes use of React Native audio and animation features, and uses AsynchStorage to persist high scores. Scan the QR code in the live-link below to try the game out.
